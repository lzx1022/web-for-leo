OSCEOLA PERGOLA
Coral Gables, FL, USA

Prof. Frank Martinez

Lake Osceola, located centrally at the University of Miami, is the campus’s most prominent natural amenity(although originally built out of a quarry) which affords a tranquil environment amidst busy campus life. In order to accentuate a connection between the land and water, I propose to build a pavilion and place of repose and contemplation by the water. Among the most important purposes of my design is to encourage the University community to experience the lake as closely as possible, to serve as a place for contemplation and quiet gathering, and to be accessible to all.
