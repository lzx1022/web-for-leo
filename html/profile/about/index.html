Leo Liu
Born in Hangzhou, China.

In 2009, Leo went to University of Miami School of Architecture to pursue BA degree honors. During the period between 2009-2014, he took the opportunity to explore different architectural study and practice methodologies around the globe. The experiences gained on historical architectural preservation in Rome, Italy and contemporary master planning study in Nantes, France.

In summer of 2012, Leo went back to China and worked as an architectural intern at Pan-China Construction Group, Wuhan and CCDI Group, Shanghai. He continued to work as an intern at Keenen/Riley Architects in Miami during the summer of 2013 and mainly worked on a single family residence project located in Coconut Grove, Miami. Leo is currently working as a full time architectural designer at Robert A.M. Stern Architects in New York City.
